Ant Farm

Visualise the movement of your ants on an ant farm.

First of all, you need to create an ant farm by dragging the rooms around.

You can either add or delete the room.

+ for adding the room and - for deleting the room.

NOTE: Orange rooms are the main rooms between which movement will be done, therefore they cannot be deleted.

Then select two rooms to create connection between them.

- also for deleting the connection.

After creating the farm just select the number of ants to go through the farm.

Finally, press Run to see your ants in action!
